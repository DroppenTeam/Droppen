Welcome to Droppen the Gaming Platform!

We want everyone to have fun and stay safe while playing games on our platform. To help ensure this, we have some rules that everyone must follow:

  1. No exploiting or cheating allowed. This includes using hacks, bots, or any other third-party software to gain an unfair advantage.
  2. No bullying or harassing other players. Be respectful and kind to everyone.
  3. No sharing personal information, such as your full name, address, or phone number. This is to protect your privacy and safety.
  4. No online dating or romantic relationships allowed. Our platform is for gaming and having fun, not for finding love.
  5. No sharing inappropriate content or language. Keep everything clean and kid-friendly.
  6. No discrimination or hate speech allowed. We welcome players of all backgrounds and identities.

By following these rules, we can all have a fun and safe gaming experience together. Enjoy!
